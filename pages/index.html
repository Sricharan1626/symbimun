What is MUN?

The Model United Nations Conference is a simulation of the United Nations. With an insight into currently significant issues, MUNs are the best tools in the making of a globally aware citizen where delegates discuss, deliberate and tackle various matters of concern with effective knowledge in a nuanced and diplomatic manner.

It gives the delegates a chance to hone their skills by bringing together various facets like Public Speaking, Research and Critical Analysis. Conferences like these are platforms to inspire the youth to think outside the box.

About Chitkara MUN 2019

Chitkara University MUN Society proudly announces the 7th Edition of the Chitkara International MUN and the First ever Climate Change Summit.

As we aim at leading the world towards a safer and sustainable path, with our arms wide open, we invite those who are motivated by the desire to achieve, make a change and be a step ahead to participate in one of the finest conferences ever.

The Conference will be an amalgamation of five committees, each of which will be witness to intense deliberations and thought-provoking discussions. Also, we have the International Press which will be responsible for presenting the happenings of the event.
