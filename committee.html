OUR COMMITTEES

Explore the dynamic committees of SymbiMUN 2026, designed to challenge your diplomacy, negotiation skills, and global awareness.

DISEC

Disarmament and International Security Committee. The First Committee of the General Assembly dealing with disarmament, global challenges, and threats to peace.

IFI

Indian Film Industry. A specialized committee simulating the creative, regulatory, and commercial dynamics of Indian cinema.

International Press

The observer corps comprising reporters and photographers who capture the proceedings and publish newsletters.

UNSC

United Nations Security Council. One of the six principal organs of the UN, charged with ensuring international peace and security.
