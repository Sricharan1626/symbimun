Welcome to SymbiMUN

SymbiMUN is the flagship Model United Nations (MUN) conference organised by Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT), Hyderabad, offering a powerful platform for students to simulate the United Nations and engage in high-impact dialogue on global issues.

Rooted in our institution’s commitment to holistic education, global awareness, and leadership development, SymbiMUN is more than a competition—it’s a transformative educational experience.

At SymbiMUN, students step into the shoes of diplomats and policymakers, representing various countries and collaborating to resolve real-world challenges through negotiation, diplomacy, and debate.

What is MUN?

Model United Nations (MUN) is an academic simulation of the United Nations where students take on the roles of diplomats representing different countries or international organizations. Participants research global issues, debate international policies, negotiate resolutions, and collaborate with others to find practical solutions to real-world problems.

Through structured committee sessions that follow UN procedures, students gain a deeper understanding of international relations, global governance, and contemporary world affairs while engaging in respectful dialogue and problem-solving.

About the Initiative

SymbiMUN is the official Model United Nations initiative of Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Hyderabad, dedicated to fostering diplomacy, leadership, and global awareness among students. It serves as a platform where participants engage in structured debates on pressing international, political, and socio-economic issues by representing countries and global institutions.

Through high-quality committee simulations, SymbiMUN aims to cultivate critical thinking, public speaking, negotiation, and policy-making skills while encouraging respectful dialogue and collaborative problem-solving. The conference aspires to build globally conscious individuals who are confident, articulate, and prepared to take on leadership roles in an increasingly interconnected world.

About SIT Hyderabad

Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT), Hyderabad is a constituent of Symbiosis International (Deemed University), Pune. The institute offers undergraduate programs in engineering with a strong focus on academic excellence, innovation, and industry-oriented learning.

SIT Hyderabad emphasizes a holistic education through a blend of rigorous academics, practical exposure, research, and co-curricular activities. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus culture, the institute aims to nurture technically competent, socially responsible, and globally aware professionals prepared to meet the challenges of the evolving technological landscape.
